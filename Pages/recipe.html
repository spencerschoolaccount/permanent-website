Crepes

Intro paragraph

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

Step 1 In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and the eggs.
Step 2 Gradually add in the milk and water, stirring to combine.
Step 3 Beat in the salt and the butter until smooth.
Step 4 Put lightly oiled gridle or pan on medium heat.
Step 5 Pour approximately 1/4 of the batter into the pan for 1 crepe, coat the pan surface equally.
Step 6 Cook the crepe until the bottom is a light brown, flip and cook the otherside, repeat step 5 and onwards for the rest of the batter.
